# Alex

## What did I learn?
I learned how to program the M5StickC Plus2, connect hardware and software together, and create interactive features for a virtual pet. I also learned how to make a website, add a timer, and improve my problem-solving and coding skills.

## What did I build?
I built a virtual pet project using the M5StickC Plus2. The project includes a website, a timer, and several other fun and interactive features that make the virtual pet more enjoyable to use.

## What was hard and how did I overcome it?
One of the hardest parts was making all the different features work together correctly and fixing bugs in the code. Sometimes the device or website did not work as expected, so I tested my code, searched for solutions, and made small improvements step by step until everything worked properly.

# Aram

We added internet to our virtual pet on M5StickC PLUS2! Now, you can connect to the pet using a special IP address. When you open the website, you can control the pet from your phone or computer. You can feed the pet, change its name, play the Mario sound, and change the screen brightness.
The website also shows the pet's battery level and status.
When you click a button on your phone, the pet gets happy immediately.
It is very easy and fun to control the pet without touching the device.
You can look after your virtual friend from any room in your house.
We want to add more online games and features to this project soon.
